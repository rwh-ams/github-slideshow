Keep your index fingers on the E and I keys of your keyboard. Words or images representing the categories at the top will appear one-by-one in the middle of the screen. When the item belongs to a category on the left, press the E key; when the item belongs to a category on the right, press the I key. Items belong to only one category.

If you make an error, an X will appear - fix the error by hitting the other key.

Press the space bar to begin Stage 1.
